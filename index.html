Employee ATRRITION ANALYSIS A CASE STUDY OF IBM TECHNOLOGY COMPANY

In this case study, IBM a technology giant have been experiencing high levels of attrition, particularly among employees in certain departments and at certain job roles. Employee attrition can be a major challenge for organizations of all sizes and industries. Losing valuable employees can not only be a financial burden, but it can also have a significant impact on organizational culture, productivity, and overall success. In a bid to find a solution to the problem, the HR team collected data on employee attrition rates over the past five years. We also collected data on employee demographics, job roles, job levels, and other factors that might be contributing to the attrition problem.
